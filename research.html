The DarkQuest experiment is an ideal laboratory for probing dark sectors through their production and decay into SM particles. Several DM and dark sector models can be tested at DarkQuest; all scenarios share the common feature of containing a “portal” interaction with the SM that connects the SM to the dark sector. More information is contained in our Snowmass paper

My work is mainly conducting simulation based on previous setup of e1039 SpinQuest and building particle indentification filter with multiple characteristic Quantities. For more details, please check my last update slides. Besides, a python package for data analysis of DarkQuest is under construction as for fun.
