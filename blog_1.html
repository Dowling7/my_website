Presentation on Brandeis XIII SciFest

Deep Neural Network-based Particle Identification

In the 2023 edition of SciFest, I had the opportunity to present my work on a Particle Identification Algorithm, a significant progress on Track Reconstruction at DarkQuest. This presentation was a notable highlight of the event, showcasing the innovative and cutting-edge research undertaken by undergraduates at Brandeis.

SciFest, an annual celebration organized by the Division of Science, showcases undergraduate research conducted in Brandeis laboratories during the summer. This poster session, held in early August at the Shapiro Science Center, marks the culmination of summer research projects. The event attracts a diverse audience, including faculty, postdoctoral researchers, research staff, administrators, graduate students, friends and family of the presenters, and other members of the Brandeis community.
